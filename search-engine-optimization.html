What is SEO?

SEO is the process of optimizing a website for search engines to find it.

Why is SEO important?

Most visitors will find your site through the use of a search engine, so using SEO will help with getting your site to appear near the top of search engine results and getting your site known.

What are some SEO techniques?

Some methods for improving SEO include: using a descriptive and relevant title tag, using the description meta tag, and using a descriptive URL structure. The title and description is what users see in search engine results, so having relevant and descriptive information will help users find what they are looking for. A descriptive and simple URL structure helps search engines crawl your documents as well as providing more visually appealing links for users.
